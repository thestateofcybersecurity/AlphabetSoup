Certified Information Privacy Professional/United States (CIPP/US)

Definition

The Certified Information Privacy Professional/United States (CIPP/US) is a designation offered by the International Association of Privacy Professionals (IAPP) that focuses on U.S. privacy laws and regulations, including the privacy principles of the Fair Information Practice Principles (FIPPs) and major laws such as HIPAA, COPPA, and the CCPA.
